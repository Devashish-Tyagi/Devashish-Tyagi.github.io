Extempore Speech Contest

The word extemporaneous means “without planning”. However, an extemporaneous speech is actually a well-prepared speech that relies on research, clear organization of statements, and practiced delivery. It is neither read nor memorized, so it is never delivered exactly the same way. Since extempore speech composed almost entirely in the moment, so it requires higher level of spontaneity, extensive research and preparation.

The Elements of an Effective Extemporaneous Speech :

 Speak About Something Worth Saying
 Speak with Sincerity
 Speak with Your Own Style

Description :

  1. Team Size : 1
  2. Delivery Time : 1 to 3 Minutes

CAD Builder

CAD or computer-aided design and drafting (CADD), is technology for design and technical documentation, which replaces manual drafting with an automated process. It help to draft construction documentation, explore design ideas, visualise concepts through photorealistic renderings and simulate how a design performs in the real world. The competition gives an opportunity to designers to showcase their capability innovation and design skill.

Description :

  1. CAD modelling competition is based on Computer aided designing software. We will provide a related pdf drawing. Participants need to draft same drawing on the Auto CAD.

Rules and Regulations :

  • Maximum 1 participant per team.
  • PDF drawing will be given at the time of competition.
  • Points are noted for drafting, detailing, labelling and colouring
  • The final result will be judged on the basis of completeness and optimum time.

Bridge Champ

Bridge Champ is an opportunity to put your wisdom and experience together to showcase your skills to make a bridge. This event is designed to allow students the opportunity to develop a Bridge that will be tested for strength-to-weight ratio.Bridge will be loaded at centre of the span for which provision to be made by the participant. Judgment will be based on deflection and load bearing capacity of the bridge model.

Rules :

  • Bridge Champ is a Spot event.
  • Maximum of 2 participants are allowed in a team.
  • A bridge is to be made with ice-cream sticks. Participants will bring their own materials for making the bridge .
  • The minimum height of sticks should be 10cm, maximum width of construction is 10cm, length(span) of the Bridge is 50cm
  • Participants should complete the task within stipulated time.

Home Automation

Home automation is a automation of home with pre-programmed smart devices to fulfil the particular demands of the residents. Centralized control of lighting, security checks/locks for home access, HVAC control, and others. Home automation is also known as smart home automation, which is an automation system, which offers access to control appliances and devices such as lights, fan, alarm system, heating & cooling systems, and electrical outlets with a smartphone. An automated home provides numerous benefits to end-users, such as reduced energy spending, security from theft, and protection from unauthorized access to the home. The solutions are gaining popularity in the residential and hospitality sectors, to protect their personal and commercial assets such as buildings, hardware infrastructures, personal belongings, and others
It remotely monitors and controls your home using the Internet of Things. Participants are invited to imagine one aspect of a home and create its prototype.
